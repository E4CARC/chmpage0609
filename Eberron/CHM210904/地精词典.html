地精语辞典Goblin Glossary

本词汇表包含之前章节中使用的常见地精语词汇和短语的定义，以及一些额外术语，玩家和GM可利用这些增加角色扮演的风味。

 

*译注：当你看完这个词典，除了能看懂关于达贡的介绍里的一堆咒语般的地名——比如路坎卓尔(Rhukaan Draal，意为皇冠之城；瓦拉卓尔(Volaar Draal，意为言语之城))和氏族名(路卡塔什氏族Rhukaan Taash，意为剃刀皇冠；凯斥沙拉特氏族Kech Shaarat，意为持剑者；凯斥瓦拉氏族Kech Volaar，意为持言者；you get it)，还会发现科瓦雷大陆上很多名字以“卓尔”（draal，地精语“城市”）结尾的地点都建立在曾经高度发达的地精城市的废墟之上——哪怕它们并不在现在的达贡境内，比如卓姆的索萨卓尔(Suthar Draal)，卡萨克卓尔(Cazhaak Draal)，阴影湿地的塔兰卓尔(Taarn Draal，意为精灵之城)；当然，这并不是说名字里没卓尔的就不会曾经是地精城市，有的名字已经改变，比如萨恩），甚至卓姆的3位鬼婆统治者的称谓“索拉(Sora)”也是地精语中“受尊敬的年长女性”的意思（如果不是同音的巧合的话）；

BTW. 不，瑞依卓尔(Riedra)与地精城市的卓尔(draal)无关，只是中译的巧合罢了。

词性PARTS OF SPEECH

 

te. 

"He/him," masculine pronoun.

"他"，阳性人称代词。

nu. 

"She/her," feminine pronoun.

"她"，阴性人称代词，

mu. 

"They/them," neutral pronoun.

"他/他们"中性人称代词（译注：包括单数和复数）。

ya. 

"I/me," first person singular.

"我"，单数第一人称代词。

kaas. 

"We/us," first person plural. See also kaas in Numbers and General.

"我们"，复数第一人称代词。另见其在《数字》下的意义。

ta. 

"You," second person singular.

"你"，单数第二人称代词。

taan. 

"You all," second person plural.

"你们"，复数第二人称代词。

mi. 

"One," indefinite pronoun, generic you.

"某人"，不定代词，"你"的通用说法。（译注：类比英语中的不定代词：anything, everyone, nobody这类；"mi"相当于one的地位）

gath-. 

"Not," "without," negative prefix; for example, gath'kal—without end.

"不"、"无"，否定前缀；例：gath’kal——没有终结。

pa-. 

Prefix indicating something given.

前缀，表示某物是被给予的。

-mo. 

Suffix indicating intensity.

后缀，表示强烈程度。

常用语COMMON PHRASES

 

atta so?

"What's that?"

"那是什么？"

ban.

"Sure," "your funeral," noncommittal agreement.

"行吧"，"你说是就是吧"，不置可否地表示同意。

chaat'oor.

"Defiler," pejorative term for species not native to Khorvaire, especially humans. Compare to gath'dar.

"亵渎者"，对非科瓦雷原住民种族的蔑称，尤其指人类。与gath’dar比较。

cho.

"Yes," "okay," acknowledgment of a statement, implied agreement.

"是的"，"OK"，对陈述表示认可，隐含同意的态度。

gath'dar.

"Not dar," a general term for any humanoid that's not dar. Compare to chaat'oor.

"非达尔人"，指代任何非达尔人的类人生物的一般称呼。与chaat’oor比较。

khaavolaar!

"Blood of the word!" A curse of frustration or amazement.

"言之血啊！"，表示沮丧或惊诧的咒骂。

maabet!

An ancient curse whose meaning has never been translated into Common.

一句古老的咒骂，其意义从未被成功地翻译为通用语。

marhu orr!

"Emperor's ears!" An exclamation of surprise.

"帝王的耳朵啊！"表示非常震惊的感叹词。

mazo.

"Absolutely," "yes," emphatic agreement; used when discussing plans or acknowledging orders.

"当然如此"，"尊命"，强调同意的态度，用于商讨计划或接受命令的场合。

saa.

"Hello," casual greeting.

"哈喽"，非正式的问候语。

saa'atcha.

"It's an honor to meet you," formal greeting.

"很荣幸见到您，"正式场合的问候语

ta muut.

"You have honor," "you do your duty." The most common form of thanks in Dhakaani culture, it acknowledges that a deed has been performed correctly without any connotation of debt on the part of the speaker. Contrast with Ya panozhii kita atcha.

"荣誉属于你"，"你完成了你的责任"。这是达坎文化中最常见的表示感谢的说法，它承认一件事被正确地执行了，同时暗含说话者对此不带有任何亏欠的意思。与ya panozhii kita atcha形成对照。

taat.

A derogatory, insulting term for someone of lesser status than the speaker.

对比说话者地位低的对象的带有贬低、侮辱性的称呼。

ya panozhii kita atcha.

"I owe a debt to your honor," an expression of profound indebtedness; this has deep meaning and is never said lightly. Compare with ta muut.

"我欠你的荣誉一个人情"(I owe a debt to your honor)，这是一种深刻的表达亏欠的方式；这个说法具有很深的含义，从不会被轻易地说出口。与ta muut形成对照。

 

军衔MILITARY RANKS

 

marhu.

"Emperor," supreme leader; a position first held by the duur'kala Jhazaal Dhakaan.

"帝王"(emperor)，最高领袖；该职位最早由挽歌咏者贾泽尔·达坎(the duur'kala Jhazaal Dhakaan)担任。

lhesh.

"High warlord," general. In Dhakaani culture, a lhesh is a term for someone with the rank of general, given command of a significant portion of the empire's armies for a set time. In the modern day, Lhesh Haruuc Shaarat'kor has adopted the term as the title of the ruler of Darguun.

"高级战争领主"(High warlord)，将军(general)。在达坎文化中，lhesh是对具有将军地位的人的称呼，其在规定的一段时间内被授予帝国军队的极大部分的指挥权。在现代，拉什·哈洛克·沙拉特·寇(Lhesh Haruuc Shaarat'kor)采用这个词作为达贡统治者的称号(译注：意译为”大将军”)。。

lhevket.

"Elder warlord," colonel.

"元老战争领主"(Elder warlord)，上校(colonel)。

lhevk'rhu.

"Skilled warlord," captain.

"熟练战争领主"(Skilled warlord)，上尉(captain)。

lhevk.

"Warlord," lieutenant.

"战争领主"(Warlord)，中校(lieutenant)。

lhurusk.

"War leader," sergeant.

"战争领袖"(War leader)，中士(sergeant)。

thradaask.

"Shock trooper," private; most often guul'dar.

"突击兵"(Shock trooper)，列兵/二等兵(private)，通常是大地精(guul'dar)

olhirot.

"Spear-bearer," private.

"持矛者"(Spear-bearer)，列兵/二等兵(private)

rhu.

Prefix or suffix indicating higher status, experience, or prestige than others of that rank

前缀或后缀，表示比该军衔的其他人具有更高的地位、经验或声望。

*译注：对美国的军衔没有概念，总结这些军衔由高到低为：lhesh > lhevket > lhevk'rhu > lhevk > lhurusk > thradaask ~= olhirot。

 

一般术语GENERAL TERMS

*译注：有些词的音译在其他译文中多次出现，比如kech凯斥(氏族)，kor寇(猩红)，sharaat沙拉特(刃)，draal卓尔(城)，为了方便在看译文时便于联想原意，这些词的解释会在开头用<>标上音译，其他的就省去音译了。

 

aath.

"Corner," "wall." See also aath in Numbers.

"角落"，"墙壁"。也见数字中的aath。

aram.

"Wrath," "righteous anger."

"盛怒"，"正义的愤怒"。

atcha.

The Dhakaani concept of personal honor, which must be earned and carefully protected. Compare with muut.

达坎人概念中的个人荣誉，必须赢得并小心守护。与Muut相对照。

atchot.

Look someone in the eye.

看着某人的眼睛。

ban'na.

"I don't care," "none of your business." A Lhazaar explorer once asked the native goblins what their exotic yellow fruit was called; a misunderstanding led to the word "banana" being adopted into Common. Literally, "not mine, your funeral."

"我不在乎"，"不关你的事"。一位拉扎尔探险家曾经就一种黄色水果叫什么名字询问过当地的地精；这个误解导致"香蕉(banana)"这个词被采纳进通用语。字面意义是，"不是我的，是你的葬礼"。

batuuvk.

"Marketplace."

"集市"。

calabas.

"Kennel."

"犬舍"。

chib.

"Boss," "big person." For non-dar, this can refer to any larger humanoid.

"老大"，"大人物"。对于非达尔人来说，这可以指任何较大体型的类人生物。

chit guulen pamuut ran.

"There is strength in honoring a sacrifice."

"尊重牺牲是一种力量"。

chot.

"Eye."

"眼睛"

chot'uul.

"Dream watcher," a monastic orderthat monitors and protects the Uul Dhakaan, or a member of that order.

[叙特'兀尔]"守梦人"，指监视和守护兀尔·达坎(Uul Dhakaan)的僧侣组织，或指该组织的成员。

daashor.

A dar artificer. The techniques of the daashor were previously believed to have been lost with the fall of the Dhakaani Empire, but have been preserved among the Kech Dhakaan.

[达叙尔]一位达尔奇械师。曾经，人们认为达叙尔(daashor)的技艺已经随着达坎帝国的衰落而失传，但达坎部落将其保留了下来。

daask.

"Soldier."

"士兵"。

dar.

"People," the collective term for the hobgoblin, bugbear, and goblin races of Dhakaan.

[达尔]"人民"，是对达坎的的大地精、熊地精和地精种族的统称。

dor.

"Never," eternal negation. This follows the negated term; "gan dor" means "never eat."

"从不"，永恒的否定。跟在被否定的词之后；比如"gan dor "的意思是 "永不吃"。

doovol.

"Truth."

"真相"。

draal.

"City."

[卓尔]"城市"。

draguus.

"Dragon."

"龙"。

duum.

A large goblin drum with a deep voice, typically played with short thick rods of brass or heavy wood.

一种大个的地精式鼓，声音低沉，通常用黄铜或重木制的短粗棒演奏。

duur.

"Dirge," "sorrow," can also mean "song of the past."

"挽歌"，"悲伤"，也可以指"讲述过去的歌"。

duur'kala.

"Dirge singer." Duur'kala preserve the history and knowledge of past ages, and their music is the most common form of magic among the Dhakaani. As the magic manifests mostly in females, duur'kala are often called gaanu duur, "daughters of the dirge," and elders are referred to as niianu duur, "mothers of the dirge."

[杜尔’卡拉]"挽歌咏者"。杜尔’卡拉(Duur'kala)保留了过去时代的历史与知识，他们的音乐是达坎人中最常见的魔法形式。由于这种魔法主要展现在女性身上，所以杜尔’卡拉通常被称为gaanu duur，"挽歌的女儿"，而年长者则被称为niianu duur，"挽歌的母亲"。

Duur'shaarat.

"Blade of Sorrows," the modern dar name for the ruins of the hobgoblin metropolis that now form the foundations of the city of Sharn. Compare to Ja'shaarat.

"悲伤之刃"，是现代达尔人对构成现代萨恩城地基的曾经的大地精都会的废墟的称呼。对照"光明之刃"(Ja'shaarat)。

gaa'ma.

"Wax baby," a pejorative term for changelings.

"蜡崽子"，这是一个对幻身灵(changelings，3R译半变形怪)的蔑称。

gaanu.

"Daughter."

"女儿"。

gaanu duur.

"Daughter of the Dirge," an alternate title for duur'kala.

"挽歌的女儿"，是挽歌咏者(duur'kala)的别称。

gaa'taat.

"Less than a child," an insult.

"不如一个孩子"，一种辱骂。

gaate.

"Son."

"儿子"。

gan.

"Eat."

"吃"。

gantii.

"Hungry."

"饥饿"。

gath'atcha.

"Without honor." This can be said as a means of showing contrition, but is a serious insult when said of someone else.

"没有荣誉"。这句话可以用来表达悔恨；但如果是用来说别人，就是一种严重的侮辱。

ghaal.

"Mighty," with specific connotations of prowess in battle.

"伟大的"，有特指战斗力的含义。

ghaal'dar.

"Mighty people," the ancient name for the hobgoblin species; also the name of the confederacy of goblin clans dominating the nation of Darguun.

[嘉尔达尔]"伟大之民"，是对大地精种族的古称；也是目前支配达贡的诸地精裔氏族联盟的名称。

Ghaal'duur.

"Mighty Dirge," a powerful horn fashioned by Jhazaal Dhakaan.

"[嘉尔杜尔]"伟大挽歌*"是贾泽尔·达坎(Jhazaal Dhakaan)制作的一支具有强大力量的号角。

*前译：嘉尔杜尔，最终挽歌(Ghaal'duur,the Mighty Dirge)"

golin.

"Quick," typically referring to speed, but can also meanintelligence.

"快"，通常指的是速度，但也可以指智慧。

golin'dar.

"Quick people," the ancient name for the goblin species.

"迅捷之民"，是对地精种族的古称。

guul.

"Strong."

"强壮"。

guul'dar.

"Strong people," the ancient name for the bugbear species.

"强壮之民"，是对熊地精种族的古称。

guulen.

"Strength." The name of a legendary artifact also known as the Rod of Kings.

"力量"。一件传说中的神器的名字，它也被称为国王之杖。

haakar.

"Panther."

"豹"。

ja.

"Bright."

"光明"。

Ja'shaarat.

"Bright Blade," the ancient name of the hobgoblin metropolis on the Dagger River that later served as the foundations of the city of Sharn. Compare to Duur'shaarat.

"光明之刃"，是匕首河畔的大地精都会的古称，其后来成为萨恩城的地基。对照"哀伤之刃"(Duur's shaarat)。

Jhazaal Dhakaan.

The greatest duur'kala in history, she united the Six Kings to found the Empire of Dhakaan and become the first marhu. She created the Uul Dhakaan and crafted Ghaal'duur and other legendary artifacts.

[贾泽尔·达坎]历史上最伟大的挽歌咏者(duur'kala)，最初正是她团结六位国王、创建了达坎帝国，并成为了第一位帝王(Marhu)。她创造了兀尔’达坎(Uul Dhakaan)，制作了嘉尔杜尔(Ghaal'duur)，以及其他传说中的神器。

kaas.

"Hand." See also kaas in Numbers and Parts of Speech.

"手"。另见《数字》和《词性》下的kaas。

kaan.

"Helmet," or more loosely, any head covering.

"头盔"，或者更宽泛地说，任何头部复盖物。

kaana.

A signal of urgency. Emphasized by repetition—kaana kaana.

一个紧急的信号。通过重复强调，比如"kaana kaana"。

kala.

"Singer."

"歌者"。

kapaa.

"Betrayal," "treachery."

"背叛"，"变节"。

Kapaa'vola.

"The Treacherous Word." Created by the daelkyr Dyrrn the Corruptor, this devastating curse severed the surface dar from the Uul Dhakaan and caused irrational behavior.

"背叛之言"。这个毁灭性的诅咒由异变魔腐化者·迪恩(Dyrrn the Corruptor)所创造，正是它切断了地表的达尔人与兀尔·达坎(Uul Dhakaan)之间的联系，并使前者陷入疯狂。

karda.

"Throne."

"王座"。

kech.

"Bearer," "clan."

[凯斥]"持...者"、"氏族"。

Kech Dhakaan.

"Keepers of Dhakaan," also translated "Heirs of Dhakaan," the collective title for the dar clans that fled from the Kapaa'vola and maintained Dhakaani traditions.

"达坎的守护者"，也译为 "达坎后裔(Heirs of Dhakaan)"，是对从背叛之言(Kapaa'vola)诅咒下逃脱、并保持了达坎传统的达尔氏族的统称。

khaar.

"Blood."

"血"。

khaar draguus.

"Blood of the dragon," an ancient Dhakaani term for the lava pools used to forge weapons and armor, now beneath the modern city of Sharn.

"龙血"，是古代达坎人对用于锻造武器和盔甲的熔岩池的称呼，现位于现代城市萨恩的地下。

khesh.

"Silent."

"寂静"。

Khesh'dar.

"The Silent Folk," a clan of golin'dar scouts and assassins. By ancient tradition, the Khesh'dar have always lived apart from the dar of the empire and serve no single clan, instead acting as impartial mercenaries.

"寂静之民"，一支由迅捷之民(golin'dar)斥候和刺客们组成的氏族。根据古老的传统，寂静之民总是与帝国的其他人民分开生活，他们总是作为不偏不倚的中立雇佣兵，不效忠任何单个氏族。

kiirin.

A traditional Dhakaani stringed instrument.

一种传统的达坎弦乐器。

kor.

"blood red," "scarlet."

[寇]"血红"，"猩红"。

korluat.

A highly alcoholic beverage; literally, "hero's blood."

一种高度含酒精的饮料；字面意思为 "英雄之血"。

kraat.

"Smithy," "forge."

"铁匠铺"，"锻造厂"。

kra'uul.

"Dream forged," items that accompany their bearer into the Uul Dhakaan.

[凯拉'兀尔]"梦铸物品"，这种物品能够伴随持有者进入兀尔·达坎(Uul Dhakaan，达坎之梦)。

kron rhukaan.

"Six Kings," the six ghaal'dar warlords who joined with Jhazaal Dhakaan to found the Empire of Dhakaan.

"六王"，即，与贾泽尔·达坎(Jhazaal Dhakaan)结盟、并一同建立达坎帝国的六位伟大之民(ghaal'dar)战争领主。

kron rhukaan'kor!

"Blood of Six Kings!" An oath of sincerity, or an expletive.

"六王之血！" 用作真诚的誓言，或用作咒骂。

kur.

"Key."

"钥匙"。

kurar.

"Death."

"死亡"。

kuur.

"Speak."

"说"。

lhesh shaarat.

"Warlord's blade," a Dhakaani weapon suitable only for the greatest warriors, finely made and imbued with powerful enchantments. The mere act of drawing a warlord's blade is a claim to power.

"战争领主之刃 "是一种只有最伟大的战士才能使用的达坎武器，制作精良，并被注入了强大的魔法。仅仅是拔刀出鞘的行为，就是对权力的主张。

lheshat.

Kingdom; literally, "the reach of a warlord's power."

王国；字面意思是"一位战争领主权力所及的范围"。

marhuat.

An empire; literally, "the reach of an emperor's power."

帝国；字面意思是 "一位帝王权力所及的范围"。

mor.

"Life."

"生命"。

morguur.

"Lifespan."

"寿命"。

mur.

"Horn."

"号角"。

muut.

The Dhakaani concept of duty, something owed to the empire and your comrades. Compare with atcha.

达坎人概念中的"责任"，是指你对帝国和你的同僚们所需负责的东西。与Atcha(荣誉)对照。

na.

Something that belongs to someone else. Can be applied as a suffix as emphasis; for example, kur'na means "Not my key."

属于别人的东西。可以作为后缀以表示强调；例如，kur'na意为 "不是我的钥匙"。

nasaar.

"Night."

[纳萨]"夜晚"。

niianu.

"Mother," nunu is a diminutive form.

"母亲"，缩略形式为nunu。

niianu duur.

"Mother of the Dirge," an alternate title for a senior duur'kala.

"挽歌的母亲"，对地位较高的挽歌咏者(duur'kala)的别称。

noon.

A starchy grain, often pressed into compact balls. Noon was a staple of the traditional Dhakaani diet, though the clans turned to suur during their long isolation.

一种富含淀粉的谷物，通常被压成紧凑的球。noon是传统达坎饮食中的主食。不过，在隔绝于地下的漫长时期中，诸达坎部落转而食用suur(译注：一种富含淀粉的真菌)。

nozhii.

"Debt."

"债"。

orces.

"Orc."

"兽人"。

paal.

"Spell."

"法术"。

paaldaask.

A spellcaster; literally, "spell warrior."

一个施法者；字面意思是 "法术战士"。

paatcha!

Literally, to offer honor. Typically spoken as a compliment, or delivered as an imperative to troops facing peril.

字面意思是，给予荣誉。通常作为赞誉之词，或作为传达给面临危险的部队下命令的用语。

piir.

General term indicating possession.

表示占有的一般用语。

piiroto.

"My stuff," one's belongings.

"我的东西"，某人的所有物。

poltaa.

"Thought."

"思想"。

raat.

"Story."

"故事"。

raat shan gath'kal dor.

"The story stops but never ends," the traditional closing of Dhakaani legends.

"故事停下了，但永远不会结束，"达坎传说故事的传统收尾。

raat shi anaa.

"The story continues," the traditional opening to Dhakaani legends.

"故事还在继续，"达坎传说故事的传统开场。

ran.

"Sacrifice."

"牺牲"。

rhukaan.

Crown; literally, "high status head covering."

[路坎]皇冠；字面意思是 "地位高的头饰"。

roo.

"Friendly stranger." Someone unknown to the speaker, but not obviously an enemy.

"友好的陌生人。" 说话人不认识的、但是不明显敌对的人。

ruuska.

"Tiger." Often used as battle mounts by Dhakaani cavalry.

"老虎"。常被达坎骑兵用作战斗坐骑。

ruuska'te.

"Tiger man," a term of endearment for a fierce man (feminine: ruuska'nu). See also taarka'nu.

"虎男"，是对勇猛的男性的爱称（阴性形式：ruuska'nu）。也见taarka'nu。

sar.

"Little."

"小"。

sehn.

"Riders."

"骑手"。

shaarat.

"Blade."

[沙拉特]"刃"。

shaarat'dor.

A martial art practiced by the Khesh'dar; literally, "no weapons."

寂静之民(Khesh'dar)所操习的一种武术，字面意思是 "没有武器"。

Shaarat'khesh.

An order within the Khesh'dar, golin'dar specializing in espionage and assassination; literally, "Silent Blades."

寂静之民(Khesh'dar)内部的一个组织，由专门从事间谍和暗杀活动的迅捷之民组成；字面意思是 "寂静之刃"。

shaat'aar.

A small sweet bun filled with honey cream, much loved by golin'dar.

一种装满蜂蜜奶油的小甜包，深受迅捷之民的喜爱。

shava.

"Sword brother," a solemn bond between dar warriors. Accepting the status of shava is a formal bond that carries significant responsibilities and expectations, trusting one another with their lives, and committing to resolve the affairs of a fallen sword brother.

"[沙瓦]"剑兄弟*"，是达尔武者之间结成的一种庄严纽带。接受沙瓦的身份意味着结下深刻的纽带，它伴随着重要的责任和期望，包括将性命托付于对方，以及当另一方牺牲时承担身后之事。

*译注：也译为"血盟卫"，详见失语翻译的《国王末日》

shi anaa.

To continue; literally, "to begin again."

继续；字面意思是，"再次开始"。

shii marhu polto huuntad ka ruuska atchot.

"Even an emperor must think twice when looking a tiger in the eye," a Dhakaani proverb.

"即使是皇帝，在看着老虎的眼睛时也必须三思而行。"一句达坎谚语。

shiftaa.

Shifters.

化兽者(3R译半兽化人)。

skai.

"Great."

"巨大的"。

skiir.

"Run."

"跑"。

skuurz'taat.

"Pathetic drunkard."

"可悲的醉鬼"。

sora.

"Grandmother," a term of respect for an elder, even if unrelated to you.

[索拉]"祖母"，对长者的尊称，即使没有血缘关系也可用。

suur.

A fungus, staple of the Dhakaani diet while the clans were deep underground, usually eaten in the form of starchy balls.

一种菌类，是达坎诸氏族遁入地下深处时期的主食，通常以淀粉球的形式食用。

taarn.

Elves.

精灵。

taarka.

"Wolf," can also refer to hounds or worgs.

"狼"，也指猎犬或座狼。

Taarka'khesh.

An order within the Khesh'dar, golin'dar specializing in wilderness reconnaissance; literally, "Silent Wolves."

寂静之民(Khesh'dar)内部的一个组织，指擅长野外侦查的迅捷之民；字面意思是 "寂静之狼"。

taarka'nu.

"Wolf woman," a term of endearment for a fierce woman (masculine: taarka'te). See also ruuska'te.

"狼女"，对勇猛的女性的爱称（阳性形式：taarka'te）。也见ruuska'te。

taash.

"Razor."

[塔什]"剃刀"。

tohiish.

"Dangerous."

"危险"。

tuuv.

"Buy," "Own."

"购买"，"拥有"。

uul.

"Dream."

"梦"。

Uul Dhakaan.

The shared dreamscape of the dar.

[兀尔·达坎，达坎之梦]达尔人所共有的梦境。

uul'kala.

"Dream singer," a dirge singer who specializes in walking in dreams.

"梦咏者"，一类擅长在梦境中行走的挽歌咏者。

uul'kur.

"Dream key," a token that allows its bearer to remember its dreams in the Uul Dhakaan, and even share with others.

[兀尔'库尔]"梦境钥匙"，这是一种能令持有者记住自己在兀尔·达坎(Uul Dhakaan)的梦的信物，甚至可以通过它与他人分享。

vanon.

"Dusk."

[凡农]"黄昏"。

volaar.

"Word," more broadly, lore or recorded knowledge.

"言语"，更广泛地说，也包括传说故事或被记录的知识。

vus.

"Fire."

"火"。

vusrii.

"Burn."

"燃烧"。
